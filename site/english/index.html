Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2025

Hörbuch‑Manager (the “App”) is provided by Bjarne Dietrich (“we”, “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the limited information that may be processed while you use the App.

1  No Data Collection

The App does not:

  • collect, or transmit personal data,
  • create user accounts or require sign‑in,
  • use third‑party analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

All audiobook files remain on your device or are streamed directly from Apple Music using Apple’s MusicKit framework. We do not have access to this content.

  • iCloud – Syncs playback progress between your devices. Data is stored in your private iCloud container; we cannot access it.

4  Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. Because no personal data is collected, parental consent is not required.

5  Security

No external servers operated by us are involved.

6  Changes to This Policy

If we change the way the App handles data, we will update this policy and note the new revision date above. A new version will never reduce your rights without explicit notice.
